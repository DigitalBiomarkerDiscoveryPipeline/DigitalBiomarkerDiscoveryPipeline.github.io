Open DBDP

Open DBDP is a partnership between Open mHealth and the Digital Biomarker Discovery Pipeline (DBDP). Combining Open mHealth's interoperable open data standard with the DBDP's platform for transforming that data into indicators of health outcomes will help accelerate research using mHealth and wearables data for digital biomarker discovery.
